NOTABLE GAMES

Over the games almost 10 year history, there have been thousands of professional games played. I wanted to highlight some of my favourite ones, and explain why in my eyes, they are extraordinary. These will be arranged in chronological order.

2012 Na’Vi vs iG, TI 2 Upper Bracket Game 2

This is one of the most famous games of Dota, ever. IG have a wombo-combo line up of heroes, with plenty of area effects and great set-up. At one moment of the game, iG catch Na’Vi completely off guard, executing their full combo on them. However, Na’Vi manage to turn the fight around in spectacular fashion, without losing a single hero.

2013 Na’Vi vs Alliance, TI3 Grand Finals, Game 5

TI3’s grand finals were the most exciting games of the game’s history. The final game in the series was a rollercoaster of emotions, with an insanely close finish.

2015 EG vs CDEC, TI5 Grand Final

One of the greatest single plays in the International’s history. 2 of EG’s supports vs all of CDEC. $6,000,000 for 1 Echo Slam.

2017 Liquid vs VP, TI7 Main Event

The back and forth, the emotions in the casters’ voices, 103 minutes of nail biting.

2018 OG vs PSG LGD, TI8 Grand Final

Down 2-1, OG need to win this game to stay in the Grand Finals. Dota Plus win probability has them at 11% chance to win.
