HISTORY AND DEVELOPMENT

Origins

Counter Strike: Global Offensive was first released on the 21st August 2012.
It is the fourth entry in the Counter Strike series and is one of the most popular multiplayer FPS titles ever. The Counter-Strike series started as a Half: Life modification and was created by Minh Le and Jesse Cliffe.
This mod quickly became very popular and in 2000 Valve, (the creators of Half Life) teamed up with the creators to release the game as a standalone title. This game is widely regarded as one of the best games of all time and was the start of a franchise of incredible games.

Original box art for Counter-Strike

There are four main entries to this series, Counter-Strike, Counter-Strike:Condition-Zero, Counter-Strike: Source and finally Counter-Strike:Global Offensive.
Each game represents a different generation of players and many debate on which is the best in the series with each having their own fanbases (some even still play the original 20 years later.)

CS:GO Development

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive was released on August 21st 2012. The game is very similar to its predecessors and even includes many of the same maps. The maps of CS:GO are actually mostly made by fans of the games and are ported in by valve to official updates.
CS:GO builds on the groundworks that the past games have built up, making new maps, new game modes such as Arms Race and Wingman. New features are still constantly being added which keeps the game fresh and enjoyable.
