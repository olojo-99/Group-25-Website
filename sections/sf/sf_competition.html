TOURNAMENTS

Street Fighter is one of the oldest and most respected fighting series in the history of the game industry.
It has a long history and a lot of fans from the whole world, which helps this game stay popular nowadays.
Street Fighter V develops actively as the esports discipline.
Developers of the game Capcom, help to organize a huge amount of tournaments anywhere they can.
What is more, Capcom organizes the Capcom Cup yearly, the biggest Street Fighter tournament with the best players from the whole world and the biggest prize pool in the season.

In the history of esports, certain highlights have managed to transcend games – plays so incredible that no fan, regardless of game loyalty, can deny their brilliance.
Think of Dota 2’s “the Play”, Coldzera's Jumping AWP in CS:GO and League of Legends’ “the xPeke”.
However, before any others is one that stands alone - a play which is arguably the original iconic esports moment and is quite possibly the most influential of them all: EVO Moment 37.
It was a close eSports match between Justin and Daigo where Daigo won by parrying perfectly with only a frame of health which takes a lot of skill, after he parried he continued with multiple attacks without fail because if he failed he would have lost the match and the whole tournament but he took the prize.
