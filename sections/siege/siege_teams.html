TEAMS

As the Rainbow Six Esports scene is a very new Esport, there are not a lot of large esports teams in the scene compared to other tier 1 esports such as Counter Strike or League Of Legends.
But this does not mean that there aren’t any large organisations in the scene.

In Europe, there are large organisations such as G2 who are in Germany, Empire who are in Russia, Vitality and BDS who are both French teams and Natus Vincere who are a team from the UK.
Out of these teams, team Vitality is the only team that has been in the scene since the game’s beta 5 years ago.
But super teams such as G2 and BDS have been in the scene for about 3 years and have helped develop the esport.

APAC, which stands for Asia Pacific, is by far the smallest and weakest region with the least exposure.
But this region has very large teams nonetheless such as; Fnatic, who are located in Australia, and Cloud9 and Nora Rengo who are both located in Japan.

In LATAM, which stand for Latin America, there are a huge number of large organisations which are all located in Brazil such as Team Liquid, NIP, Faze Clan, Team One and Immortals.
As you can see, this region has so many large teams and is a very competitive region as a result of that.
As for the NA region, out of the 8 teams that compete in this region, there are 4 teams that dominate the region so the league is very lob-sided.
These 4 teams, which all have a house in Vegas, as this region is a LAN so they don’t play online. These teams are Spacestation Gaming, Darkzero, TSM and Oxygen.
